City of Trimble

This website is your hub for information on the city. You can find most of the information on your local government, businesses, utilities, and upcoming events. Trimble is in the southwest corner of Clinton County, Missouri, centrally located on US 169 Hwy between Kansas City (south) and St. Joseph (north). The Smithville Lake Wildlife area is directly east.

Meetings

  • City Council on The Trimble Board of Aldermen meet for their regular meetings on the 1st Monday of each month, 7:00 P.M. If it falls on a holiday, it will be held the following Wednesday.
